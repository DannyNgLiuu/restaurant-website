Best pizza in New York

At Danny’s Pizzeria, we believe that great pizza starts with passion. Our handcrafted pies are made with the freshest ingredients, from our signature dough to our rich, slow-cooked tomato sauce and premium toppings. Whether you're craving a classic Margherita, a hearty meat lover’s delight, or a creative specialty pizza, every bite is packed with flavor and love.

History

Pizzeria started as a small family-owned shop with a simple goal: to serve authentic, handcrafted pizzas made with love. Founded in 2004 in Brooklyn, Danny’s journey began with a passion for great food and a commitment to using only the freshest ingredients.

What started as a cozy neighborhood spot quickly became a local favorite, known for its crispy, wood-fired crusts, rich homemade sauces, and perfectly melted cheese. Over the years, Danny’s Pizzeria has expanded, but the heart of the restaurant remains the same—delivering delicious, high-quality pizza while bringing people together.
